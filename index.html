A JOURNEY THROUGH LONGING

‘a journey through longing’ is an immersive digital concept brought to you by Stockholm-based Heima Collective. Using emotive narratives and interactive visuals, we find hope, wonder and comfort in longing for normality in a post-covid world.

For an optimal desktop experience, activate sound, navigate with mouse/trackpad and grant access to your camera.

A Hyper Island Student project made by Markus Wallén, Sarah Masete, Annika Tidén, Joanna Lundbladh, Mahad Kalusumu and Coryn Dickson
